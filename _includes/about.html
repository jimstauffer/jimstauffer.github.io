About

A quick tour of Jim Stauffer's life.

  • a long time ago

    Jim's Humble Beginnings

    In a land far away, Glendale, CA, James was born at 8lbs 10oz to Jocqueline and Maurice Stauffer

  • The 60's

    Lifeguarding

    Starting from an after school job, lifeguarding on the beautiful beaches of southern Orange County became central to who Jim is. So much in fact that he was able to create a career out of it.

  • The 60's

    Education

    Graduated from San Diego State with a degree in accounting, All while maintaining to save dousins of lives on the beaches working as a lifeguard on Orange County beaches.

  • The 60's

    Real Estate License

    After college, Jim earned his real estate license. Worked for a broker and mentored by his father who had his own real estate company. At age of 23 he owned his first house.

  • The 70's

    Lifeguard for City of Laguna Beach

    After a few year stint as working as an accountant during the recession Jim decided to return to his passion as a lifeguard. Roaming the beat in his famous red trunks for 7 years climbing the ranks to captain.

  • 1971

    First Sea Lion rescue

    Upon scouting Laguna Beach, he noticed several sea lions in distress on the beach. Taking it upon himself, he built a pool in his backyard and nursed the sea lions back to health. This renegade approach to stand up for wildlife has filtered down to his children who both have become biologists.

  • 1978

    Life Guard International

    Jim and best friend Dick Johnson decided to try their own luck at guarding the beaches of Laguna. Relying only on their instinct they entered into county politics and put a bid to protect the citizins. The duo won the bid and maintained the company with over 100 employees for 11 years. In the meanwhile, he had kept busy with realty.

  • 1974

    Friends of Sea Lions

    After spending years helping marine mammals, Jim realized he needed to officially Taking knowlegde he gained from the Army reserves vetinary division along with many many volunteers he recruited amongst the ranks of his Lifeguard company, coupled with his attitude of making things happen, in his backyard he nursed dousins of large marine mammels back to health. Legends has it that he used his 912 porche to transport sick dolphins to and from the beach. After a few years he was able to start the non-profit and has now become a Laguna Beach instituion rebranded as Pacific Marine Mammal Center.

  • 1989

    Birds of Paradise Reality Era

    Learning from his dad, he decided the next chapter of his life was to work directly with people and helping them find homes. Jim passed his broker exam. Jim also offered sound advice to both buyers and sellers, and even on occasion had to give life advice. Birds of Paradise gave the Stauffer's great success and after 3 years, the family decided to move north to Petaluma to raise their kids.

  • 1992

    Remax and James Stauffer Realty

    After moving to Petaluma, Jim began to work with Remax. After Remax, Jim again, being the entrepruer he is, started another realty company. He has now over 30 years of active real estate and broker experience. Specializing in residential, he has helped many in the North Bay find or sell homes.
